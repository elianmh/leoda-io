# Prompt recipes — Nano Banana

Plantillas probadas por categoría. Sustituye los `<placeholders>` con detalles del producto/marca antes de enviar.

Todas en inglés (Nano Banana rinde mejor en EN).

---

## 1. Hero product shot — e-commerce

```
Premium product shot of <PRODUCT>, centered composition, clean <BACKGROUND_COLOR> seamless backdrop, soft studio lighting from upper left with subtle rim light, sharp focus on product, shallow depth of field, commercial photography style, <BRAND_PALETTE> color grading, vertical 4:5 framing, ultra-high detail, no text
```

**Aspect ratio**: `4:5` (feed) o `1:1`.

---

## 2. Lifestyle / contextual shot

```
Authentic lifestyle scene featuring <PRODUCT> in use by <PERSONA_DESCRIPTION>, <LOCATION_CONTEXT>, natural <TIME_OF_DAY> lighting, candid feel, 35mm lens look, warm cinematic color grade, depth of field, subject mid-frame, no logos visible except on the product
```

**Aspect ratio**: `9:16` para Reels/TikTok/Stories.

---

## 3. Flat-lay editorial

```
Overhead flat-lay composition of <PRODUCT> surrounded by <CONTEXTUAL_PROPS>, on a <SURFACE_DESCRIPTION>, soft even daylight, minimalist arrangement, symmetric balance, editorial magazine aesthetic, muted natural palette, sharp focus throughout, square 1:1 framing
```

**Aspect ratio**: `1:1`.

---

## 4. Mockup en escena — packaging

```
Photorealistic mockup of <PACKAGING_TYPE> labeled with <BRAND_NAME>, displayed on <SURFACE>, soft directional studio light, subtle reflection on surface, <BRAND_PALETTE> color story, commercial advertising photography, hyper-detailed label texture, vertical 9:16 framing for stories
```

**Aspect ratio**: `9:16`.

---

## 5. Anuncio paid ad — alto contraste

```
Bold advertising visual: <PRODUCT> hero shot on <SOLID_COLOR_BACKGROUND>, dramatic side lighting, high contrast, ultra-saturated <ACCENT_COLOR> accents, glossy reflective surface, dynamic angle, premium beverage/tech advertising aesthetic, square 1:1 framing, clean negative space for copy on the right
```

**Aspect ratio**: `1:1` o `16:9` para LinkedIn/Facebook.

---

## 6. Branding — identidad visual

```
Brand identity mood board layout featuring <PRODUCT_OR_LOGO>, accompanying typography samples, <BRAND_PALETTE> swatches, texture references, all arranged on a neutral surface, top-down view, soft uniform lighting, editorial design portfolio aesthetic, 16:9 horizontal layout
```

**Aspect ratio**: `16:9`.

---

## 7. Anuncio TikTok — UGC look

```
Vertical 9:16 amateur smartphone-style photo of <PERSONA> holding <PRODUCT> in a <REAL_LOCATION>, natural ambient light, slight motion blur, candid expression, raw documentary feel, no studio polish, organic social media authenticity
```

**Aspect ratio**: `9:16`.

---

## 8. Lifestyle servicio (B2B / SaaS)

```
Modern office or workspace scene with <PERSONA> using <DEVICE_OR_INTERFACE>, soft natural window light, neutral palette with <BRAND_ACCENT>, professional but human atmosphere, shallow depth of field, 35mm lens, horizontal 16:9 framing
```

**Aspect ratio**: `16:9`.

---

## Tips finales

- **No mezclar muchos estilos** en un mismo prompt — pick one.
- **No pedir texto** dentro de la imagen si necesita ser preciso — Nano Banana lo dibuja borroso. Mejor compón el texto encima en post.
- **Reproducibilidad**: guarda el `seed` que devuelve fal.ai. Pasándoselo de vuelta puedes generar variantes consistentes del mismo concepto.
- **Negative prompts** se ponen con frases como `no text, no watermark, no logo, no people` directamente en el prompt — Nano Banana no tiene parámetro `negative_prompt` separado.
